WHO WE ARE

Positive Reinforcement was founded in early 2017 by Emma, Alex, and Max: three individuals with extensive experience within Melbourne's hospitality and event management sector. Collectively, we identified a demand for a specific type of crowd control officer (CCO) - a demand we felt was not being met by any existing security company.

Melbourne culture has shifted significantly in recent years, with an increased focus on creating safer spaces for more diverse crowds. Positive Reinforcement is supporting CCOs through this cultural change by providing them with additional training and a progressive working environment. This will result in better outcomes for venues, events, clients, patrons, and the CCOs themselves.

Our Goals

1. Providing CCOs to venues and events who hold professional values aligned with those of the community we serve.

2. Providing CCOs who are an asset to any venue or event, and who will work with venue owners and event promoters to achieve mutually beneficial outcomes.

3. Providing an alternative crowd control service. From paying award wages to eliminating the toxic culture of aggression and violence, we want to provide clients with the option to make a better choice.
